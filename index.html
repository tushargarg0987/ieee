All accepted and presented papers will be considered for inclusion in IEEE Xplore (indexed by Scopus) subject to the quality requirement.

Technically Sponsored by

International Conference on “Integrated Circuits, Communication, and Computing Systems (ICIC3S-2024)” is the premier forum for showcasing recent developments and research findings in the domains of integrated circuits, microwave, communication, and next generation computing. The conference will bring together leading researchers, engineers and scientists in the domain of interest from around the world. The impetus for this conference is based on the growing necessity to support an exponentially growing number of intelligent devices while also maintaining highly effective communication systems.
