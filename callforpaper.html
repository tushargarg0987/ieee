TRACK(s)

International Conference on " Integrated Circuits, Communication and Computing Systems (ICIC3S) is the premier forum for showcasing recent developments and research findings in the domains of integrated circuits , communication, and computing.The conference will bring together leading researchers , engineers and scientists in the domain of interest from around the world. The subjects that might be of interest for submission include, but are not limited to:

PAPERS ARE INVITED FOR ICIC3S 2024 FROM THE FOLLOWING AREAS
(BUT NOT LIMITED TO IT):-

1. Integrated Devices and Circuits

  • Electronic Devices
  • Analog, Digital & Mixed Signal Design
  • Low- Power, Low- Voltage Designs
  • Devices Modeling and Process simulation
  • Biosensors and Bioelectronics
  • Opto-Electronic Devices
  • MEMS, NEMS design and Technology
  • Flexible Electronics
  • Actuators, Sensing Devices
  • Wearable and Implantable Circuits
  • Smart Electronic Materials
  • Sensor technologies

2. RF, Microwave and mmWave

  • Microwave Communications
  • Advanced RF measurement techniques
  • Antenna Design for Communication Systems and Networks
  • Antennas and systems for 5G and beyond
  • Metamaterials for antennas
  • Milimeter, Terahertz antennas and systems
  • MIMO, Massive-MIMO and OFDM
  • Terrestrial, Earth-Space and Ionosheric propagation
  • Tunable and reconfigurable antennas

3. Communication and Signal Processing

  • Communication Theory and Applications
  • Algorithms and Techniques for 5G and beyond
  • Wireless sensors and Ad Hoc Networks
  • Vehicular Communications and Networks
  • Under water communication
  • Software Defined Radio
  • Cognitive Radio and Spectrum Management
  • Intelligent Protocols for Communication Systems and Networks
  • Machine-to-Machine Communications Systems and Networks
  • Signal Processing: Theory, Methods, and Applications
  • Signal Processing System and Architectures
  • Machine-to-Machine Communications
  • Adaptive Communication Systems and Networks

4. Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics & Computing

  • Computer Vision
  • Embedded System
  • Intelligent Robotics
  • Internet of Things and its applications
  • Artificial Intelligence and applications
  • Soft Computing
  • Quantum Computing
  • Neural Network and Machine Learning
  • Multimedia Signal Processing
  • Speech Signal Processing
  • Image and Video Processing
  • Biomedical Signal computing
